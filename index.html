How to Play

Sudokube is a 3D extension of classic Sudoku.

  • For each of the 6 faces, every row, column, and 3x3 box must contain the numbers 1 through 9 without repetition.
  • The key challenge is that the edges and corners are shared between faces. A number placed on an edge must be valid for both faces that share it.
  • A number placed on a corner must be valid for all three faces that meet there.
